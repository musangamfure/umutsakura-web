Our Story

Not just a caterer.
Not just a band.

UMUTSAKURA Justin Ltd. was born from a single conviction: that food and music should never be treated as afterthoughts at a Rwandan celebration. We are the country's only company to design catering and live entertainment as a unified experience — where the band's energy matches the feast, and the feast matches the occasion.

"Your only job on your wedding day is to be present. Ours is to make sure everything else is perfect."

From the centralized Kigali kitchen where our chefs begin preparation on Thursday, to the dedicated attendant who ensures the bride and groom actually eat at their own wedding — every detail is deliberate.

The Cuisine

Rwandan heritage,
honored on the plate

Our food is not fusion for fusion's sake. It is Rwanda's finest culinary tradition, elevated with modern technique and presented with the dignity it deserves. From igitoki slow-braised in aromatic spices to our signature live carving station — every dish tells a story of the land.

We over-cater by 15% as standard. The last guest at the buffet receives the same abundance as the first.

Igitoki

Slow-braised, served in banana leaf boats

Isombe

Fresh cassava leaves, crispy shallots

Brochettes

Oak-smoked, carved tableside

Live Carving

Roasted whole lamb, carved at the station
